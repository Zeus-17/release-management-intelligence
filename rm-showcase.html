⬡ Deterministic AI Intelligence
Intelligence that fires as you work.
No API. No data sent anywhere.
17 pattern rules analyse your input the moment you type — regulatory keywords, security vulnerabilities, authentication changes, database migrations, offshore dependencies. Each triggers tailored, actionable guidance specific to release management.
Release planner: payment keywords → DORA Article 9 regulatory flag. Auth changes → rollback guidance. Mobile → App Store lead time warning.
Defect triage: "crash" or "null pointer" → auto-classifies as Critical Blocker. "cosmetic" → suggests Known Issue/Deferred.
Quality gates: warns when entry criteria incomplete before sign-off. Flags open blockers at Go/No-Go.
Dashboard: CFR above 15% → DORA band warning. Active freeze window → alert. Multiple RED releases → escalation prompt.
Why deterministic, not generative?
Release management decisions affect production systems and regulated environments. Deterministic rules produce consistent, auditable, predictable outcomes — not probabilistic suggestions. Rules are updateable from the content JSON without touching the app.

Release Readiness at a glance
A 0–100 score calculated from four weighted factors: gate completion (40%), defect status (30%), environment health (20%), and sign-off progress (10%). No more "how are we looking?" — the number tells you.
Below 50 — RED. Don't go near a go/no-go conversation.
50–74 — AMBER. Decisions ahead. Blockers need addressing.
75+ — GREEN. Gate criteria met. Ready for Go/No-Go.
Score updates live as gates are signed, defects resolved, environments promoted.
